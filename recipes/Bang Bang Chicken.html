Bang Bang Chicken

Description

This delicious meal is prepared in roughly 40 minutes.The estimated time stated includes the preparation of the ingredients and the actual cooking.

All ingredients listed within are appropriate for a meal that is to be served in 4 dishes.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1/4 cup potato starch
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon shichimi togarashi (japanese red pepper condiment)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • oil for frying
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons sweet chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon gochujang (korean hot pepper paste)

Steps

  1. Whisk together milk and egg in a bowl until smooth. Add chicken to egg wash and marinate for 15 minutes.Drain.
  2. Combine potato starch, flour, salt, pepper, and ground red pepper in a bowl and transfer into a large resealable bag. Add egg-washed chicken pieces and shake until each piece is completely dredged.
  3. Heat oil in a large skillet to 325 degrees F (165 degress C).
  4. Add chicken pieces into the oil in batches, about 5 to 8 pieces at at time, so as not to drop the temperature of the oil. Fry for 90 seconds. REmove to a paper-towel lined plate to drain. FRy remaining chicken pieces.
  5. Increase oil temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). THe oil should be continously boiling at this point. carefully re-add the first batch of chicken and fry for another 90 seconds. Remove to a paper-towel lined plate to drain. Fry and drain the remaining chicken. TRansfer chicken to a medium bowl.
  6. Stir mayonnaise, honey, sweet chili sauce, and gochujang together in a small bowl. Toss fried chicken with sauce to coat.

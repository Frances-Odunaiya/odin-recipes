Better Spanish Rice

Description

This delicious meal is prepared in roughly 20 minutes.The estimated time stated includes the preparation of the ingredients and the actual cooking.

All ingredients listed within are appropriate for a meal that is to be served in 4 dishes.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon Vegetable oil
  • 12 Onion, Chopped
  • 114 uncooked instant rice
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 12 cup chopped fresh cilanrt
  • 1 cup chicken broth

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium high heat, and cook and stir the chopped onions
  2. Do above until brown. THis should take about 8 minutes
  3. Stir in the rice, tomatoes, cilantro, and chicken broth and bring to a boil
  4. Reduce the heat to medium-low, and simmer until the rice is cooked and most of the liquid is absorbed. THis should take about 10 minutes
  5. Voila! Food should be served!
